How to participate

  1. Perform some music (or find a video of music you like)! You can perform alone or with friends, or stand with your friends who are performing music.
  2. Take a photograph or video of you and your friends performing music.
  3. Donate to support those affected by Ebola (suggested organizations on the Donate page).
  4. Post the photo or video to your facebook page and challenge your friends to participate.
