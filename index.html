About the Ebola Music Challenge

The Ebola Music Challenge aims to help take part in the fight against Ebola by getting groups together to perform a piece of music in support of those affected by the disease. Take part in the challenge by organizing a concert or consider donating to one of the organizations running help efforts. See the participation page to see how you can get involved.
